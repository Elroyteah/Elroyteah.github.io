Step 1

Draw a 70x30mm rectangle

Step 2

Draw 2 circles at both ends of the rectangle using the 3-point tangent circle function

Trim the rectangle

Step 3

1) Draw a circle with diameter 5.5mm at the tip of the left circle

2) Draw another circle with diameter 4mm at the center of previous circle

Step 4

Offset the bigger design by 1.5mm and trim the excess

Step 5

Extrude the circumference by 4mm

Extrude the inner floor by 1mm

Step 6

Draw a text and place it inside the keychain

Step 7

Extrude the text by 2mm

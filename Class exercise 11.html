3D Printing Laser Cutting
Final project

Step 1

1) Import your desire design into Inkscape

2) Use Trace bitmap to trace the outline of your design

3) Export your image as SVG

Step 2

1) Import your SVG image into fusion

2) Draw a center line

3) Offset your image by 2mm

4) Draw a line at the base of the design so that we can select the blue image at shown below

Step 3

1) Revolve your design

2) The profile should be the blue selected area shown in step 2 and the axis should be the center line

Final design

Step 4

1) Export Final design as STL file

2) Import the design into Ultimaker Cura

3) Change the settings and slice the design

3D Printed design

1) Wait for the 3D printed piece to cool

2) Remove support and clean the piece

Final project

Step 1

Prepare the breadboard and a powersupply

Step 2

1) Find all the components we need,
2 DIP switches, 8 220ohm resistors and a seven-segment LED display

2) Important to connect the cathode of the DIP switch to the anode of the resistors

Step 3

Connect the wiring

1) Connect the VCC and GND of the power supply to the negative and positive circuit of the breadboard, black for GND, red for VCC

2) Connect the VCC of the breadboard to the anode of the DIP switch

3) Connect the LEDs of the seven segment to the cathode of the resistors, LED A to most left and DP to most right

Step 4

Test the design by flipping the DIP switches

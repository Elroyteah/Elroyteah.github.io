3D Printing Laser Cutting
Final project

Step 1

1) Draw a 70x30mm rectangle

2) Draw circles at both ends of the rectangle using the 3-point tangent circle function

Step 2

Trim the edges of the rectangle

Step 3

1) Draw a circle with diameter 6mm at the tip of the circle

2) Draw another circle with diameter 4 at the center of the circle

3) Offset the big design by 2mm

Step 4

1) Trim the excess

2) Draw a text in the design

Step 5

Import the FABLAB logo into Inkscape

Step 6

Use the Trace bitmap function to get a black and white logo

Export black and white image as SVG

Step 7

Import the SVG image into fusion

Step 8

UnFix the image so that we can edit it

Step 9

1) Remove the FABLAB words that came with the image

2) Scale the image to fit in the keychain

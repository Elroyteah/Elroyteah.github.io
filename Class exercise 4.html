Final project

Step 1

Draw a circle with diameter 60mm and a circumsribed hexagon with diameter 20mm in the center of circle

Step 2

Draw another cicle with diameter 40mm and a inscribed pentagon with diameter 15mm in the center of circle

Step 3

Use the measure tool and spread both circles 129mm away from each other from their center

Step 4

1) Draw a rectangle of 129x20mm from center of circle to the other center

2) Trim the rectangle, leaving of the middle portion

3) Draw another rectangle of 70 by 10mm on the center of previous rectangle

Step 5

Draw 2 circles at the side of the small rectangle using 3-point tangent circle

Step 6

Trim the corners of the rectangle and the excess circle

Step 7

Extrude the bigger circle by 20mm, leaving out the hexagon

Step 8

Extrude the center design by 15mm

Step 9

1) Extrude the center design by 5mm from the bottom

2) Extrude the smaller circle by 20mm, leaving out the pentagon

Step 10

Extrude the smaller circle by 2.5mm from the bottom

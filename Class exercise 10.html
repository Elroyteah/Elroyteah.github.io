3D Printing Laser Cutting
Final project

Step 1

Start by drawing a rough sketch of your laptop stand

Step 2

Adjust your design to your desired length using the dimension function

Step 4

1) Add 'thickness' into user parameters

2) This should be according to the material you are going to cut with,
in this case, it is a 3mm plywood

Step 5

Add a second user parameter called 'Legwidth'

Step 6

Extrude the design by 'thickness'

Step 7

1) Copy the design

2) Paste the design and spread it 'Legwidth' apart

Step 8

1) Test out if the user parameters are working by changing the values of the measurements

Step 9

1) Draw a stand on top of the legs

2) Extrude the stand by 'thickness'

Step 10

1) Combine the stand and the legs together using the combine function

2) Select the appropriate target body and tool body and the operation should be cut

Step 11

Check if the legs are cut correctly

Step 12

Check if your use parameters are working after combining the stand

Step 13

Draw a leg support and add an offset plane at the midpoint of the design

Step 14

Mirror the leg support from the offset plane

Step 15

Combine the leg support with the leg by following step 10
